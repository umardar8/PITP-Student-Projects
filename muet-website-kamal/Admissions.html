Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • Intermediate (Pre-Engineering) with minimum 60% marks
  • Entry Test (MUET Entry Test/NTS)
  • Matriculation with minimum 60% marks
  • Domicile of Sindh Province (for Sindh quota)

Postgraduate Programs

  • B.E./B.Sc. Engineering with minimum 2.5 CGPA
  • GAT (General) with minimum 50% score
  • Departmental Test/Interview
  • Two recommendation letters
