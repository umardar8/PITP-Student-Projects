About MUET

Excellence in Engineering & Technology Since 1976

Our History

Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET) was established in 1976 as a campus of the University of Sindh. It became an independent university in 1977 and has since grown to become one of Pakistan's premier institutions for engineering education.

The university is named after the Mehran region of Sindh, reflecting its commitment to serving the local community while maintaining international standards.

Mission & Vision

Vision

To be a world-class university recognized for excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation.

Mission

To produce highly competent engineers and technologists through quality education, cutting-edge research, and strong industry linkages that contribute to societal development.

Achievements

  • Accredited by Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC)
  • International collaborations with 50+ universities
  • 300+ research publications annually
  • Over 25,000 alumni worldwide
