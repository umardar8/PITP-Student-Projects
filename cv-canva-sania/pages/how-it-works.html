What is an ATS Friendly CV?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. It is software used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies to filter resumes before a human ever sees them.

An "ATS Friendly" CV is designed to be easily readable by these bots. It avoids complex tables, graphics, and unreadable fonts. Our "Simple" and "Bold" templates are specifically engineered to pass these filters with a high score.

Do's
  • Use standard headings (Experience, Education).
  • Use clear, readable fonts.
  • Save as PDF.
Don'ts
  • Don't use columns or tables for layout (unless coded correctly).
  • Don't use graphics to display skills (like progress bars).
