Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CV Canvas. We prioritize your privacy above all else. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect or store your personal data.

CV Canvas is a Client-Side Application. This means:

  • When you type your name, phone number, or employment history, that data stays in your browser's memory (RAM).
  • Your data is never sent to a server or database.
  • Once you close the tab or refresh the page, your data is wiped (unless your browser has an autofill feature enabled).

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use local storage only to save your theme preference if applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

We use standard Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load libraries such as Bootstrap and FontAwesome. These providers may see your IP address when fetching these files, but they do not have access to the data you type into the CV forms.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact Sania via the email provided in the footer.
